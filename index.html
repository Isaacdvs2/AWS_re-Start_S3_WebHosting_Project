OUR HERITAGE

The Story of Zani’s Eatery

Established in 2026, Zani’s Eatery represents the summit of gourmet street food and flame-grilled excellence on the Waterfront. We merge authentic local marinades with premium, meticulously sourced cuts of Wagyu beef and organic free-range poultry. Our kitchen operates under a strict code of discipline to ensure every signature burger, platter, and shake is presented with outstanding culinary poise.
